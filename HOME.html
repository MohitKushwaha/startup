Why NamePlate?
We are here to help you find the right people for your next project. We make things easy & quick, and serve expert suggestions from all circles of your organisation.
We want people to meet, collaborate, learn and invent. We believe in safeguarding your privacy and entrust ourselves with keeping it within your level of organisation.
What Can You Do With NamePlate ?
Sit & Relax and let us find the right people for you.
Meet new people endowed/loaded with different set of skills.
Build up a team and kick-start your projects.
Spread words about the project you'r working on.
Know your professional community better.
Promote yourself as a rising star in your Organisation.
